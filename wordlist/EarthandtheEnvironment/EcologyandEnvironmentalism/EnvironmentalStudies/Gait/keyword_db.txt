gait 
/ gāt/
•
n.
a person's manner of walking:
the easy gait of an athlete.
 ∎ 
the paces of an animal, esp. a horse or dog.


•
v. [intr.]
(of a dog or horse) walk in a trained gait, as at a show:
the dogs are gaiting in a circle.
