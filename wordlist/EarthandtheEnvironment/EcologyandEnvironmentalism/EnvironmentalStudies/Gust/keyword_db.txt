gust 
/ gəst/
•
n.
a brief, strong rush of wind.
 ∎ 
a burst of something such as rain, sound, or emotion:
gusts of rain lashed down the narrow alleys.


•
v. [intr.]
(of the wind) blow in gusts:
the wind was gusting through the branches of the tree.
