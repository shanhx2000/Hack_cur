[
hy·a·line 
/ ˈhīəlin; -ˌlīn/
•
adj.  Anat.  &amp;  Zool. 
having a glassy, translucent appearance.
 ∎ 
relating to, consisting of, or characterized by hyaline material.


•
n.
1.
(the hyaline)  poetic/lit. 
a thing that is clear and translucent like glass, esp. a smooth sea or a clear sky.
2.
another term for hyalin.
