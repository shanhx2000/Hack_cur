[
hag1 
/ hag/
•
n.
1.
a witch, esp. one in the form of an ugly old woman (often used as a term of disparagement for a woman):
a fat old hag in a dirty apron.
2.
short for hagfish.


DERIVATIVES: 
hag·gish
adj.
hag2 
•
n.  Scot.  &amp;  N. English 
1.
(also peat hag)
an overhang of peat.
2.
a soft place on a moor or a firm place in a bog.
