[
col·lec·tor 
/ kəˈlektər/
•
n.
a person or thing that collects something, in particular: 
 ∎ 
a person who collects things of a specified type, professionally or as a hobby:
book collectors.
 ∎ 
an official who is responsible for collecting money owed to an organization or body:
a tax collector.
 ∎ 
 Electr. 
the region in a bipolar transistor that absorbs charge carriers.
