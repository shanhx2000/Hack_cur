cal·o·rif·ic 
/ ˌkaləˈrifik/
•
adj.  chiefly Brit. 
relating to the amount of energy contained in food or fuel:
she knew the calorific contents of every morsel.
 ∎ 
(of food or drink) containing many calories and so likely to be fattening.


DERIVATIVES: 
cal·o·rif·i·cal·ly
/ -ik(ə)lē/ adv.
