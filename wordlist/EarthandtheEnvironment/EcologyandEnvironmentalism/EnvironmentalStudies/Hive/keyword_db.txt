[
hive 
/ hīv/
•
n.
a beehive.
 ∎ 
the bees in a hive.
 ∎ 
a thing that has the domed shape of a beehive.
 ∎ fig. 
a place in which people are busily occupied:
the kitchen became a hive of activity.


•
v. [tr.]
place (bees) in a hive.
 ∎  [intr.]
(of bees) enter a hive.
