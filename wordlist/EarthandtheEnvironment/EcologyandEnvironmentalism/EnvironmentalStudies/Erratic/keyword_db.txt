[
er·rat·ic 
/ iˈratik/
•
adj.
not even or regular in pattern or movement; unpredictable:
her breathing was erratic.
 ∎ 
deviating from the normal or conventional in behavior or opinions:
neighbors were alarmed by increasingly erratic behavior.


•
n.
(also erratic block or boulder)  Geol. 
a rock or boulder that differs from the surrounding rock and is believed to have been brought from a distance by glacial action.

DERIVATIVES: 
er·rat·i·cal·ly
/ -(ə)lē/ adv.
