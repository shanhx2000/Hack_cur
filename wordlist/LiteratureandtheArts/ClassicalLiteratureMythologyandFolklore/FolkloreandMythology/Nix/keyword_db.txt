[
nix1 
/ niks/  inf. 
•
n.
nothing:
apart from that, nix.

•
interj.
expressing denial or refusal:
“I owe you some money.” “Nix, nix.”

•
v. [tr.]
put an end to; cancel:
he nixed the deal just before it was to be signed.

nix2 
•
n.
(fem.  nix·ie
/ ˈniksē/ )
(in Germanic mythology) a water sprite.
