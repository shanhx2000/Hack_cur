[
pix·ie 
/ ˈpiksē/
(also pix·y)
•
n.
(pl.  pix·ies)
a supernatural being in folklore and children's stories, typically portrayed as small and humanlike in form, with pointed ears and a pointed hat, and mischievous in character.

DERIVATIVES: 
pix·ie·ish adj.
