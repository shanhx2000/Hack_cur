[
pol·i·cy1 
/ ˈpäləsē/
•
n.
(pl.  -cies)
a course or principle of action adopted or proposed by a government, party, business, or individual:
the administration's controversial economic policies |
it is not company policy to dispense with our older workers. 
 ∎ archaic 
prudent or expedient conduct or action:
a course of policy and wisdom.


pol·i·cy2 
• n.
(pl.  -cies)
1. a contract of insurance: they took out a
joint policy. 2.
an illegal lottery
or numbers game.   ,
