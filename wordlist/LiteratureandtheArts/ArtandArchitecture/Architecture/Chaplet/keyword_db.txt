chap·let 
/ ˈchaplət/
•
n.
1.
a garland or wreath for a person's head.
2.
a string of 55 beads (one third of the rosary number) for counting prayers, or as a necklace.

3.
a metal support for the core of a hollow casting mold.


DERIVATIVES: 
chap·let·ed
adj.
