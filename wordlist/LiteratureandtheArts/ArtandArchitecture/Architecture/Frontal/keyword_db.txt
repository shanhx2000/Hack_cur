[
fron·tal1 
/ ˈfrəntl/
•
adj.
of or at the front:
the frontal view misses the octagonal tower.
 ∎ 
(of an attack) delivered directly on the front, not the side or back:
a frontal assault upon the Iraqi fortifications.
 ∎ 
of or relating to the forehead or front part of the skull:
the frontal sinuses.


DERIVATIVES: 
fron·tal·ly
/ ˈfrəntl-ē/ adv.
fron·tal2 
•
n.
a decorative cloth for covering the front of an altar.
