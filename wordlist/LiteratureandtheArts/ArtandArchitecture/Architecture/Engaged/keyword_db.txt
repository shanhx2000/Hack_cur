en·gaged 
/ enˈgājd/
•
adj.
1.
busy; occupied:
I told him I was otherwise engaged.
 ∎ Brit. 
(of a telephone line) unavailable because already in use.

2.
having formally agreed to marry.

3.
 Archit. 
(of a column) attached to or partly let into a wall.
