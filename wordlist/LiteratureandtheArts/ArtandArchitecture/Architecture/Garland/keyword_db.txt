[
gar·land 
/ ˈgärlənd/
•
n.
a wreath of flowers and leaves, worn on the head or hung as a decoration. 
 ∎ dated 
a prize or distinction.
 ∎ 
 archaic 
a literary anthology or miscellany.


•
v. [tr.]
adorn or crown with a garland:
they were garlanded with flowers.
