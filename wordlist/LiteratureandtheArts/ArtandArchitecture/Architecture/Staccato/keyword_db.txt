[
stac·ca·to 
/ stəˈkätō/  chiefly Mus. 
•
adv. &amp; adj.
with each sound or note sharply detached or separated from the others:
[as  adj.]
a staccato rhythm. Compare with legato, marcato.

•
n.
(pl.  -tos)
performance in this manner.
 ∎ 
a noise or speech resembling a series of short, detached musical notes:
her heels made a rapid staccato on the polished boards.
