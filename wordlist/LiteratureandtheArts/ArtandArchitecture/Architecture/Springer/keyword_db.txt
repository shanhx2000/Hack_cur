[
spring·er 
/ ˈspringər/
•
n.
1.
(usu. spring·er span·iel)
a small spaniel of a breed originally used to spring game. There are two main breeds, the
English springer spaniel, typically black and white or brown and white, and the less common red and white
Welsh springer spaniel.
2.
 Archit. 
the lowest stone in an arch, where the curve begins.

3.
a cow or heifer near to calving.
