ar·chi·trave 
/ ˈärkiˌtrāv/
•
n.
1.
(in classical architecture) a main beam resting across the tops of columns, specifically the lower third entablature.
2.
the molded frame around a doorway or window.
 ∎ 
a molding around the exterior of an arch.
