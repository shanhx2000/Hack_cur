[
am·bu·la·to·ry 
/ ˈambyələˌtôrē/
•
adj.
relating to or adapted for walking.
 ∎ Med. 
able to walk; not bedridden:
ambulatory patients.
 ∎ 
 Med. 
relating to patients who are able to walk:
an ambulatory care facility.
 ∎ 
movable; mobile:
an ambulatory ophthalmic service.


•
n.
(pl.  -ries)
a place for walking, esp. an aisle around the apse or a cloister in a church or monastery.
