[
in·ta·glio 
/ inˈtalyō; -ˈtäl-/
•
n.
(pl.  -glios)
a design incised or engraved into a material:
the dies bore a design in intaglio.
 ∎ 
a gem with an incised design.
 ∎ 
any printing process in which the type or design is etched or engraved, such as photogravure or dry point.


•
v.
(-glioes, -glioed) [tr.] [usu. as  adj.] (intaglioed)
engrave or represent by an engraving:
a carved box with little intaglioed pineapples on it.
