[
hand·i·craft 
/ ˈhandēˌkraft/
•
n. (often handicrafts)
a particular skill of making decorative objects by hand:
the traditional handicrafts of this region |
teachers of drawing, design, and handicraft |
[as  adj.]
handicraft workshops. 
 ∎ 
an object made using a skill of this kind:
pottery and handicrafts decorate the rooms and hallways.
