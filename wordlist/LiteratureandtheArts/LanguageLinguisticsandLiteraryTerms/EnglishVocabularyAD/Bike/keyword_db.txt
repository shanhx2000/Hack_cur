bike 
/ bīk/  inf. 
•
n.
a bicycle or motorcycle:
 I'm going by bike [as  adj.]
a bike ride.

•
v. [intr.]
ride a bicycle or motorcycle:
we hope to encourage as many people as possible to bike to work [as  n.] (biking)
the terrain is perfect for biking.
