[
de·flec·tor 
/ diˈflektər/
•
n.
a device that deflects something, in particular:
 ∎ 
a plate or other attachment for deflecting a flow of air, water, heat, etc.
 ∎ 
an electrode in a cathode-ray tube whose magnetic field is used to deflect a beam of electrons onto a screen to form an image.
