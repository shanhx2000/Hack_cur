[
coun·try·man 
/ ˈkəntrēmən/
•
n.
(pl.  -men)
1.  Brit. 
a person living or born in a rural area, esp. one engaged in a typically rural occupation.
2.
a person from the same country or region as someone else.
they trust a fellow countryman.
