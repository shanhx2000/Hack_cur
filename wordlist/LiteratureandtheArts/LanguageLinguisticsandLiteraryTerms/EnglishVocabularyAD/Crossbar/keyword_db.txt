[
cross·bar 
/ ˈkrôsˌbär/
•
n.
a horizontal bar fixed across another bar or between two upright bars, in particular:
 ∎ 
(in sports) the bar between the two upright posts of a goal.
 ∎ 
the horizontal metal bar between the handlebars and saddle on a man's or boy's bicycle.
