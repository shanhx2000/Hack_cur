[
de·lir·i·ous 
/ diˈli(ə)rēəs/
•
adj.
in an acutely disturbed state of mind resulting from illness or intoxication and characterized by restlessness, illusions, and incoherence of thought and speech.
 ∎ 
in a state of wild excitement or ecstasy:
a delirious crowd.


DERIVATIVES: 
de·lir·i·ous·ly
adv.
