[
am·pli·fy 
/ ˈampləˌfī/
•
v.
(-fies, -fied) [tr.] (often be amplified)
increase the volume of (sound), esp. using an amplifier.

 ∎ 
increase the amplitude of (an electrical signal or other oscillation).
 ∎ 
cause to become more marked or intense:
policy initiatives amplified social polarization.
 ∎ 
 Genetics 
make multiple copies of (a gene or DNA sequence).
 ∎ 
enlarge upon or add detail to (a story or statement).


DERIVATIVES: 
am·pli·fi·ca·tion
/ ˌampləfiˈkāshən/ n.
