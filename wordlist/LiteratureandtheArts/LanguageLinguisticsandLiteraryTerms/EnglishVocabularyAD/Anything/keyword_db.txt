[
an·y·thing 
/ ˈenēˌ[unvoicedth]ing/
•
pron.
used to refer to a thing, no matter what:
nobody was saying anything.
 ∎ 
used for emphasis:
I was ready for anything.
 ∎ 
used to indicate a range:
he trains anything from seven to eight hours a day.


PHRASES: 
anything but
not at all (used for emphasis):
he is anything but racist.

(as) — as anything inf. 
extremely—:
she said it out loud, clear as anything.
if anythingsee if.
like anythingsee like1 .
or anything  inf. 
added as a general reference to other things similar to the thing mentioned:
no strings attached, you don't have to join up or anything.
