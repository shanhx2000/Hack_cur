come·back 
/ ˈkəmˌbak/
•
n.
1.
a return by a well-known person, esp. an entertainer or sports player, to the activity in which they have formerly been successful.
 ∎ 
a return to fashion of an item, activity, or style:
stirrup pants have made a comeback.

2. inf. 
a quick reply to a critical remark.
 ∎ 
the opportunity to seek redress.
