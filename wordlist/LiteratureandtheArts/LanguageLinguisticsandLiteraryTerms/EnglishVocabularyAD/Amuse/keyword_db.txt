[
a·muse 
/ əˈmyoōz/
•
v. [tr.]
1.
cause (someone) to find something funny; entertain:
he made faces to amuse her.
2.
provide interesting and enjoyable occupation for (someone):
the hotel has planned many activities to amuse its guests.


DERIVATIVES: 
a·mus·ed·ly
/ -zidlē/ adv.
(in sense 1).
