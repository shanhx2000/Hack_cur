[
brink 
/ bringk/
•
n.
an extreme edge of land before a steep or vertical slope:
the brink of the cliffs.
 ∎ 
a margin or bank of a body of water:
the brink of the pond.
 ∎ 
a point at which something, typically an unwelcome or disastrous event, is about to happen:
a hapless dictator teetering on the brink.


PHRASES: 
on the brink of
about to experience something, typically a disastrous or unwelcome event:
the country was on the brink of a constitutional crisis.
