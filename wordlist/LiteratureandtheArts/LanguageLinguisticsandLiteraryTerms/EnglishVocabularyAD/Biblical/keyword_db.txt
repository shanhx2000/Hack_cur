[
bib·li·cal 
/ ˈbiblikəl/
(also Bib·li·cal)
(abbr.: bibl. or Bibl.)
•
adj.
of, relating to, or contained in the Bible:
the biblical account of creation
biblical times.
 ∎ 
resembling the language or style of the Bible:
there is a biblical cadence in the last words he utters.
 ∎ 
very great; on a large scale:
they see themselves as victims of almost biblical dimensions.


DERIVATIVES: 
bib·li·cal·ly
/ -ik(ə)lē/ adv.
