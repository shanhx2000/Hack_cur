[
alt·az·i·muth 
/ alˈtazəmə[unvoicedth]/
•
n.
1.
(also altazimuth mount or mounting)  Astron. 
a telescope mounting that moves in azimuth (about a vertical axis) and in altitude (about a horizontal axis). Compare with equatorial mount.
 ∎ 
(also altazimuth telescope)
a telescope on such a mounting.

2.
a surveying instrument for measuring vertical and horizontal angles, resembling a theodolite but larger and more precise.
