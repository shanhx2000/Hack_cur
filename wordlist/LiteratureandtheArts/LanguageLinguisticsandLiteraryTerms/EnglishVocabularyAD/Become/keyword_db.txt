[
be·come 
/ biˈkəm/
•
v.
(past  -came
;  past part.  -come
)
1. [intr.]
begin to be:
they became angry
it is becoming clear that we are in a totally new situation.
 ∎ 
grow to be; turn into:
the child will become an adult.
 ∎ 
(of a person) qualify or be accepted as; acquire the status of:
she wanted to become a doctor.
 ∎  (become of)
(in questions) happen to:
what would become of her now?

2. [tr.]
(of clothing) look good on or suit (someone):
the dress becomes her.
 ∎ 
be appropriate or suitable to (someone):
minor celebrity status did not become him.
