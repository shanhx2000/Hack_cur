[
col·lec·tive 
/ kəˈlektiv/
•
adj.
done by people acting as a group.

 ∎ 
belonging or relating to all the members of a group:
ministers who share collective responsibility.
 ∎ 
(esp. of feelings or memories) common to the members of a group:
a collective sigh of relief.
 ∎ 
taken as a whole; aggregate.


•
n.
a cooperative enterprise.
 ∎ 
a collective farm.


DERIVATIVES: 
col·lec·tive·ly
adv.
col·lec·tive·ness
n.
col·lec·tiv·i·ty
/ kəˌlekˈtivitē; ˌkälˌek-/ n.
