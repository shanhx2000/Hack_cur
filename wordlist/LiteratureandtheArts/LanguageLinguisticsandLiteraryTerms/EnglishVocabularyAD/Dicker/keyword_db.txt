[
dick·er 
/ ˈdikər/
•
v. [intr.]
1.
engage in petty argument or bargaining:
she advised him not to dicker over the extra fee.
2.
treat something casually or irresponsibly; toy with something:
[as  n.] (dickering)
there was no dickering with the lyrics.
