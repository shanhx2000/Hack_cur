[
a·long·side 
/ əˈlôngˈsīd; əˈläng-/
•
prep.
(also alongside of)
close to the side of; next to:
she was sitting alongside him
[as  adv.]
the boat came alongside. 
 ∎ 
together and in cooperation with:
a care assistant was working alongside him.
 ∎ 
at the same time as or in coexistence with:
alongside the development of full-time courses there had to be provision for the part-time student.
