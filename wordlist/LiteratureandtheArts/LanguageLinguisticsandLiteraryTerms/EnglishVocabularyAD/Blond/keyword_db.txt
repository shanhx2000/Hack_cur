[
blond 
/ bländ/
•
adj.
(of hair) fair or pale yellow:
short-cropped blond hair
her hair was dyed blond.
 ∎ 
(of a person) having hair of a fair or pale yellow color:
a slim blond woman.
 ∎ 
(of wood and other substances) light in color or tone:
a New York office full of blond wood.


•
n.
a person with fair hair and skin.

DERIVATIVES: 
blond·ish adj.
blond·ness
n.
