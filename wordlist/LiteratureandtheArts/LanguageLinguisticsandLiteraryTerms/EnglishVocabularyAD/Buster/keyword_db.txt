[
bust·er 
/ ˈbəstər/
•
n.  chiefly inf. 
1.
a person or thing that breaks, destroys, or overpowers something:
[in  comb.]
the drug's reputation as a flu-buster.
2. inf. 
used as a mildly disrespectful or humorous form of address, esp. to a man or boy:
your parents' decisions affect you, like it or not, buster.
