[
Al·ex·an·dri·an 
/ ˌaligˈzandrēən/
•
adj.
of or relating to Alexandria in Egypt.
 ∎ 
belonging to or akin to the schools of literature and philosophy of ancient Alexandria.
 ∎ 
(of a writer) derivative or imitative rather than creative; fond of recondite learning.
